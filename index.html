GPA CALCULATOR

Welcome to our website, where calculating your academic performance has never been easier! We understand the importance of keeping track of your grades, and that's why we've created this tool to help you calculate your SGPA and CGPA effortlessly.

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is a measure of your academic performance in a single semester, whereas CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) reflects your overall academic performance throughout your academic career.

With our user-friendly interface, you can easily input your grades and credit hours for each semester, and let our calculator do the rest. Our tool will provide you with accurate calculations for both your SGPA and CGPA, allowing you to monitor your academic progress and make informed decisions about your future.

Whether you're a student, a parent, or an educator, our GPA calculator is an essential tool for everyone who values academic excellence. So why wait? Start calculating your GPA today and take the first step towards achieving your academic goals!
